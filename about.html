About

Hi, I'm Harry Towers.

I write about software, design, and the way ideas connect across disciplines. I'm interested in how complex things can be made simple, and why simple things are often harder to build than complex ones.

This blog is where I think out loud. Some posts are finished arguments; others are half-formed questions I'm working through. I try to label which is which, but I don't always succeed.

If you want to get in touch, you can find me on the internet. I'm not hard to find.
