Hello, World

Every blog starts with a first post. This is mine.

I've been meaning to write in public for years. I'd draft something, decide it wasn't good enough, and quietly delete it. Eventually I realized that waiting until you have something perfect to say is just another way of never saying anything at all.

So here we are.

What this is

This is a place for thinking out loud. I'm interested in software, design, how people learn things, and the strange intersection of all three. Most posts will probably be short. A few might be longer. I'm not committing to a schedule — only to honesty and a genuine attempt to be useful.

The goal is not to be prolific. The goal is to write things worth reading.

If something here is useful to you, I'd love to hear it. If something is wrong, I'd love to hear that even more.
